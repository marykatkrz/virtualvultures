Virtual Vultures

The Virtual Vultures have always been primarily focused on learning the art of formation flying and sharing it with others. We routinely train pilots in the formation fundamentals and techniques. If you are interested in learning from us or just want to meet up and fly with us, you can contact us here or through our Facebook page. We will always make time for people who share our passion, regardless of skill level or experience.

We hope to see you soon in the virtual skies.
